    What is Avainpolku Oy?

    Avainpolku Oy recruits mainly young people and forwards their great skills and labour force to client companies, that want or need new labour force fast.

    Avainpolku Oy strives to improve the employment and work experience of young Finnish people and reduce the possibility of social exclusion.

    What about adults?

    Avainpolku Oy also offers work for other age groups. However, this is a little less supported than for young people, because we want to be the key to young people's working lives.
